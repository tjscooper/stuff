# 24-Week Gamified Workout Program

An interactive, gamified fitness web application that guides you through a comprehensive 24-week workout program. Track your progress, earn achievements, level up, and build a stronger, healthier you!

## Features

### Comprehensive 24-Week Program
- **Structured progression**: From foundation building to advanced power training
- **3 workout days per week** with strategic rest days built in
- **Progressive overload**: Workouts increase in intensity as you advance
- **Recovery weeks**: Every 4th week is a deload week for optimal adaptation
- **Varied training**: Full body, upper body, lower body, core, and cardio workouts

### Gamification System
- **Experience Points (XP)**: Earn 50 XP for each completed workout
- **Level System**: Progress through levels as you gain XP
- **Achievements**: Unlock 10 unique achievements for various milestones
- **Streak Tracking**: Build consistency with daily workout streaks
- **Visual Progress**: See your journey with stats and progress bars

### Achievements to Unlock
- First Steps - Complete your first workout
- Week Warrior - Complete Week 1
- Consistency - 3-day streak
- Dedicated - 7-day streak
- Rising Star - Reach Level 5
- Champion - Reach Level 10
- Half Way There - Complete 12 weeks
- Ultimate Warrior - Complete all 24 weeks
- Committed - Complete 25 workouts
- Unstoppable - Complete 50 workouts

### User-Friendly Interface
- **Responsive design**: Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile
- **Dark theme**: Easy on the eyes during workout planning
- **Interactive workout cards**: Click to see detailed exercise information
- **Week navigation**: Easily browse through all 24 weeks
- **Persistent progress**: Your data is saved locally in your browser

## How to Use

1. **Open the app**: Simply open `index.html` in your web browser
2. **View your dashboard**: See your current level, XP, streak, and achievements
3. **Navigate weeks**: Use the Previous/Next Week buttons to browse the program
4. **Start a workout**: Click on any workout card to see the detailed exercise list
5. **Complete workouts**: Click "Complete Workout" to earn XP and track progress
6. **Track achievements**: Watch as you unlock achievements and level up
7. **Build consistency**: Keep your streak alive by working out regularly

## Program Structure

### Phase 1: Weeks 1-4 (Foundation)
Focus on proper form, basic movements, and building a foundation

### Phase 2: Weeks 5-8 (Hypertrophy)
Building muscle mass with moderate weights and higher volume

### Phase 3: Weeks 9-12 (Strength)
Increasing strength with heavier weights and lower reps

### Phase 4: Weeks 13-16 (Hypertrophy)
Continued muscle building with advanced techniques

### Phase 5: Weeks 17-20 (Strength)
Advanced strength training protocols

### Phase 6: Weeks 21-24 (Power & Conditioning)
Peak performance combining strength, power, and endurance

## Technical Details

- **Pure JavaScript**: No frameworks required, lightweight and fast
- **Local Storage**: Progress saved automatically in your browser
- **Responsive CSS**: Beautiful on any screen size
- **No backend needed**: Everything runs client-side

## Tips for Success

1. **Be Consistent**: Try to work out at the same time each day
2. **Rest Days Matter**: Don't skip rest days - recovery is crucial
3. **Form Over Weight**: Always prioritize proper form over heavy weights
4. **Track Your Progress**: Use the app daily to maintain your streak
5. **Stay Hydrated**: Drink plenty of water before, during, and after workouts
6. **Listen to Your Body**: If something hurts (not just difficult), rest or modify
